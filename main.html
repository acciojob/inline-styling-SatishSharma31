Tulips are spring-blooming flowers that are part of the Tulipa genus and are related to lilies. Tulips have large, showy flowers that are usually red, orange, pink, yellow, or white. They often have a different colored blotch at the base of the tepals.

A rose is either a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa, in the family Rosaceae, or the flower it bears. There are over three hundred species and tens of thousands of cultivars.
